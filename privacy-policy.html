🔗

2. Google Account & Drive Integration

HRease PH offers an optional Google Drive integration that allows you to back up and sync your HR and payroll data to your personal Google Drive storage. This feature requires you to authorize HRease PH to access your Google Account.

OAuth Scopes Requested:

openid email profile drive.file

What each scope is used for:

  • openid, email, profile — Used to identify your Google Account so HRease PH can associate your backup files with your account. We read your name and email address only for display purposes inside the app.
  • drive.file — Grants HRease PH access only to files that HRease PH itself creates in your Google Drive. We cannot read, modify, or delete any other files in your Drive.

What we do with this access:

  • Create and update backup files (e.g., hrease_backup.json) in your Google Drive
  • Restore your data from those backup files when you request it
  • Display your Google account name/email inside the app for confirmation

What we do NOT do:

  • We do not access files you did not create with HRease PH
  • We do not upload your data to any PrimeBytes server
  • We do not share your Google data with any third party
  • We do not use your Google data for AI training or analytics

You can revoke HRease PH's access to your Google Account at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions.

📊

3. Data We Collect

HRease PH is a locally-installed desktop application. All your HR and payroll data (employee records, payroll computations, attendance, etc.) is stored only on your device — not on our servers.

We may collect the following limited information:

  • Google Account info (name, email) — only if you enable Google Drive sync, and only stored locally on your device
  • License key information — to verify your product license
  • Support communications — emails or messages you send to our support team

We do not collect:

  • Employee personal data stored inside HRease PH
  • Payroll figures or financial records
  • Usage analytics or telemetry data
  • Device fingerprints beyond what is needed for license verification
🛡️

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data stays on your device. HRease PH stores all business data in a local database on the computer where it is installed. When you enable Google Drive sync, encrypted backup files are stored in your own Google Drive account — not on any PrimeBytes server.

We recommend that you:

  • Keep your device and operating system updated
  • Use a strong password on your Windows/device account
  • Enable Google Drive sync regularly to maintain off-site backups

PrimeBytes Soluzione is not responsible for data loss caused by hardware failure, unauthorized device access, or accidental deletion by the user.
